About

AS204994 is a research network used to learn about BGP, Networking, Peering, AnyCast and VPN. We use Ubuntu Linux as out main OS, Bird as our main routing engine, Zerotier for interconnections and Git for configuration files.

Peering

Peering Info is available on Peering DB or you can email me at peering [AT] as204994 [dot] net. Our peering policy is Open. Peers may only send traffic to destinations announced by AS204994. Peers may only send traffic originating from their own / customer networks. Peers are not allowed to point static routes towards AS204994. Peers must operate a NOC which is reachable by phone or email. Peers should cooperate in any case of abuse.
